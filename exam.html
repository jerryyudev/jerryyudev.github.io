There are several ways to effectively prepare for the high school entrance examination. Here are some tips that might help you:

1. Create a practical study plan and stick to it. Planning is the art of the possible, so make sure to create a plan that is realistic and achievable. Spend more time executing your plan than planning it.

2. Know your strengths and weaknesses. Identify the areas you need to work on and focus on them.

3. Use fewer books for theory. Instead of reading multiple books, choose one or two that cover the topics you need to study.

4. Read the questions cautiously. Make sure you understand the questions before answering them.

5. Plan your exam strategy. Decide how much time you will spend on each section of the exam and stick to your plan.

6. Train your mind for the exam. Practice meditation or deep breathing exercises to help you stay calm and focused during the exam.

7. Practice previous year papers. This will help you get familiar with the format and content of the exam
.
8. Use cheat-sheets to revise formulas. Create cheat-sheets for important formulas and equations to help you revise them quickly.

Remember to start studying early and seek help if you need it. Also, make sure to have realistic expectations and don't put too much pressure on yourself. Good luck!üëç
